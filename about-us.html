Why We Built CloudMatch

Nowadays, most people play games in their bedrooms, against strangers on the internet. We set off trying to find a way to change that, and bring the idea of 'playing together' back to being next to each other.

This started the creating process that evolved into CloudMatch, a fascinating new way of interaction between devices. Communication becomes more simple and intuitive, yet secure.

We're thrilled about the opportunities that this platform will enable, and looking forward to apps designed for gaming, sharing, learning and collaborating!
