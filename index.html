Features

CloudMatch technology enables the most intuitive and humanes interactions between devices.

Cross platform

The platform doesn’t matter -- all you really need is a touchscreen and an internet connection. We’re starting out with Android and iOS, but more platform are to come. Windows? Firefox OS? You name it.

Fast connection

Once devices are matched, we create a fast and safe channel between them. Exchange any data you like!

Position recognition

Devices matched in a group will be aware of their position in the group, and you can use this information to create fancy effects.

Continuous screen

Know an exact location where two screens are being paired up. You can display one image beyond single screen.
