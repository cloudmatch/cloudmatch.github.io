developers

CloudMatch delivers an open-source technology called GestureMatch to developers and technology companies. We are committed to provide a reliable and immediate deployment of the technology at an affordable price.

GestureMatch is an open source technology invented by Fabio Triticco, a co-founder of CloudMatch. GestureMatch is released under the Apache license.
